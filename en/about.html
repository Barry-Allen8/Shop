The BIGUN team

BIGUN is a modern employment and HR advisory agency. We connect Polish employers with qualified specialists from Eastern Europe and guarantee fully legal cooperation.

Who we are

Our story and mission

We launched in 2017 as a small group of recruiters. Today BIGUN supports dozens of production, logistics and service companies across Poland.

Partnerships in Ukraine and Belarus help us reach the best candidates quickly and fill staffing gaps faster than traditional hiring models.

Every employee goes through a multi-stage selection and receives training, language support and a development path.

Our mission is to build safe, long-term relations between employers and employees through transparency and proactive communication.
