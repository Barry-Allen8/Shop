Work in Poland right away

BIGUN supports candidates from Ukraine, Belarus and beyond in finding legal jobs in Poland. Regardless of your background, we will guide you, take care of paperwork and make sure your start is comfortable.

Call or write: +48 881 017 295 · jobs@bigun.agency

How it works

Three simple steps to legal employment

We keep the process fast and transparent. Our team stays with you from the first call to signing the contract and checking into your accommodation.

01

Choose an offer

Browse open vacancies on our website or email your CV to jobs@bigun.agency.

02

Talk to a recruiter

We discuss job requirements, contract type and all documents needed to start work.

03

Sign & travel

Sign the agreement in our Bydgoszcz office or during a local info meeting. We organise transport and housing.

Relocation support tailored to you

BIGUN coordinators help with tickets, housing reservations and border paperwork. We handle every detail so your journey to Poland feels safe and organised.

What you get

Support package for BIGUN candidates

Joining BIGUN means more than a job offer. We look after your comfort, safety and future development.

Residence legalisation

We complete applications, book appointments and help you obtain work permits or residence cards.

Housing & transport

We arrange accommodation near the workplace and organise travel to Poland and to your first shift.

Dedicated coordinator

Your personal coordinator answers questions, helps you adapt and reacts to any issues throughout the contract.

Training & growth

We provide HSE induction, language courses and vocational training to boost your qualifications.

Documents

Prepare before you arrive

Your recruiter will send a detailed checklist. Most often we ask for the following items:

Passport & photos

A valid passport with visa (if required) and recent photos for official applications.

Work certificates

References, welding books, UDT licences or other certificates depending on the position.

Emergency contact

Details of a trusted person we can reach in case of an urgent situation.

Need advice?

Message our recruitment team

We will find a role that fits your skills and help you relocate to Poland safely.
